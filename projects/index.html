    7. Deep Convolutional Neural Network for Thorax Disease Detection

      • Trained a multi-label deep CNN architecture for detecting 14 types of thorax diseases based only on the chest X-ray data on a dataset consisting of 112,120 x-rays from 30805 patients.
      • Experimented with imgage trasformation for data augmentation, handling imbalanced classes using weights, droputs, ensembled and transfer learning.
      • Achieved an accuracy of 93.49% and a recall of 81%.
